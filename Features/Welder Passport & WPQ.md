# Welder Passport & WPQ

**Description**: Management of welder qualifications (Welder Performance Qualification) to ensure only certified personnel work on critical joints.

## Data Integration
- **Personnel**: Employees managed in [[HR & Workforce Module]].
- **Technical Specs**: Qualification standards defined in [[Welding & WPS Module]] (ASME IX, ISO 9606).
- **Compliance**: Expiry alerts trigger notifications in the [[Analytics & KPI Module]].
- **Operations**: Linked to the **Joint Register** to prevent unauthorized assignment.

## Key Attributes
- Welder Stamp Number.
- Process (GTAW, SMAW, etc.).
- Material & Thickness Range.
- Expiry Date & Continuity Log.
