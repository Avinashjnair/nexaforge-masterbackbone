# Job Costing & Project P&L

**Description**: Real-time financial tracking of project expenses against the quoted budget.

## Data Sources
- **Committed Costs**: Derived from [[Purchase Orders]] in the [[Procurement Module]].
- **Actual Material Costs**: Derived from [[GRN Processing]] in the [[Store & Inventory Module]].
- **Labour Costs**: Derived from timesheets and [[Utilisation Tracking]] in the [[HR & Workforce Module]].
- **Overheads**: Allocated based on the [[Overhead Absorption]] model.

## KPIs
- **Budget vs. Actual (BVA)**.
- **Gross Margin %**.
- **Cost Variance**.
