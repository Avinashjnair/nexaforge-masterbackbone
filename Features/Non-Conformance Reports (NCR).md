# Non-Conformance Reports (NCR)

**Description**: Documentation of any material, process, or product that does not meet the specified engineering standards.

## Cross-Module Impact
- **Quality**: Initiated in [[Quality Control Module]] upon detection.
- **Operations**: Triggers a "Stop Work" or "Quarantine" in [[Production Module]].
- **Finance**: Costs for rework or replacement materials are logged as [[COPQ (Cost of Poor Quality)]] in the [[Finance & Job Costing Module]].
- **Analytics**: Frequency and root cause analysis tracked in [[Analytics & KPI Module]].

## Workflow
1. Identification & Quarantine.
2. Root Cause Analysis (RCA).
3. Disposition (Use-as-is, Rework, or Scrap).
4. Corrective Action & Close-out.
