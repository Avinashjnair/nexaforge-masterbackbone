# COPQ (Cost of Poor Quality)

**Description**: Financial quantification of non-conformance and appraisal costs.

## Components
- **Internal Failure Costs**: [[Non-Conformance Reports (NCR)]], Rework, Scrap.
- **External Failure Costs**: Warranty claims, client-site repairs.
- **Appraisal Costs**: QC Inspection, NDT Testing, Third-party lab PMI.
- **Prevention Costs**: Welder training, WPS development, Quality audits.
