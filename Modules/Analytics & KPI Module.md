# Analytics & KPI Module

**Purpose**: High-level performance monitoring and data-driven decision support.

## Key Features
- **Executive Scorecard**: Headline metrics for the entire portfolio.
- **OEE Engine**: Calculation of machine effectiveness.
- **COPQ Analysis**: Tracking the Cost of Poor Quality.
- **KPI Heatmap**: 12-week performance visualization.

- [analytics.js](file:///d:/Claude%20Projects/ERP/Sprint%201/js/analytics.js): KPI definitions and trend logic.
