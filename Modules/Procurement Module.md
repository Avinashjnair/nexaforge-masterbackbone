# Procurement Module

**Purpose**: Supplier management and automated purchasing workflows.

## Key Features
- **PR Automation**: Purchase Requisition generation from Production requirements.
- **Supplier Scorecards**: Evaluation of vendor performance (quality, lead time).
- **PO Management**: Full lifecycle of Purchase Orders from draft to approval.
- **Approval Workflows**: Multi-level management authorization for expenditures.

## Technical Components
- [procurement.js](file:///d:/Claude%20Projects/ERP/Sprint%201/js/procurement.js): Vendor databases and PO logic.
- [procurement.css](file:///d:/Claude%20Projects/ERP/Sprint%201/css/procurement.css): Tables for tracking requisition status.
