# HR & Workforce Module

**Purpose**: Management of skilled labour, certifications, and shift scheduling.

## Key Features
- **Welder Passports**: Tracking of WPQ certifications and expiry alerts.
- **Skills Matrix**: Visual representation of workforce capabilities.
- **Shift Scheduling**: Weekly management of production shifts.
- **Utilisation Tracking**: Analysis of direct vs. indirect labour hours.

## Technical Components
- [hr.js](file:///d:/Claude%20Projects/ERP/Sprint%201/js/hr.js): Employee data and certification logic.
- [hr2.js](file:///d:/Claude%20Projects/ERP/Sprint%201/js/hr2.js): UI renderers for matrix and schedules.
- [hr.css](file:///d:/Claude%20Projects/ERP/Sprint%201/css/hr.css): Module-specific styling.
