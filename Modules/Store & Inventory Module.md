# Store & Inventory Module

**Purpose**: Traceability and management of raw materials and consumables.

## Key Features
- **Heat Number Traceability**: Full material pedigree for pressure-containing parts.
- **GRN Processing**: Goods Received Note generation linked to POs.
- **Quarantine Management**: Isolated storage for non-conforming or pending materials.
- **Remnant Tracking**: Management of cut plates and unused material stock.

- [inventory.js](file:///d:/Claude%20Projects/ERP/Sprint%201/js/inventory.js): Inventory ledger and traceability logic.
