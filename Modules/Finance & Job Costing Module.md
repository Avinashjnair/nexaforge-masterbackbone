# Finance & Job Costing Module

**Purpose**: To provide real-time visibility into project profitability and financial health.

## Key Features
- **Job Costing**: Automated tracking of P&L per project ([[Master Roadmap]]).
- **Milestone Billing**: Integration with production milestones for automated invoicing.
- **AR/AP Ledger**: Real-time management of receivables and payables.
- **Overhead Absorption**: Accurate allocation of facility and management costs.
